About BP Landscapes

Transforming outdoor spaces across Kenya since 2015

Our Beginning

BP Landscapes was founded on the belief that exceptional outdoor spaces start with design. From the beginning, our focus has been on understanding how landscapes function, how people move through them, and how outdoor environments connect to architecture and lifestyle. Every project begins with careful planning, site analysis, and creative problem-solving before any construction takes place. This design-first approach allows us to shape spaces that are cohesive, purposeful, and built for long-term performance.

Over time, BP Landscapes has grown noted not just for craftsmanship, but for the clarity and intention behind every project we deliver. By leading with design and executing with precision, we ensure every space we create is not only beautifully built, but thoughtfully conceived from the ground up.
