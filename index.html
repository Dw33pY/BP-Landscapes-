About BP Landscapes

Transforming outdoor spaces across Kenya since 2015

Professional Landscaping & Design

BP Landscapes is a professional landscaping and landscape design company based in Nairobi, Kenya. We offer landscape design and installation, garden and sod installation, lawn mowing and maintenance, gutter cleaning, pesticide and fertilizer application, and complete outdoor beautification. Our services also include fence installation (cedar and vinyl), Rain Bird sprinkler systems, tree removal and trimming, retaining walls, paver patios, decks, pergolas, and gazebos. We are committed to quality workmanship, sustainable practices and customer satisfaction, delivering beautiful and functional outdoor spaces for residential and commercial clients.
